QI GONG MORNING FLOW — TTS EXERCISE SCRIPTS
================================================

Full session: ~30 minutes | 7 exercises | All levels
Voice: calm, slow, warm. Pauses marked with "..." are breath pauses.
Speak at about 0.85x speed for a meditative pace.

------------------------------------------------
EXERCISE 1: STANDING MEDITATION & CENTERING
Duration: 4 minutes
------------------------------------------------

Stand tall, feet shoulder-width apart. Let your arms hang heavy at your sides. Soften your knees — just a slight bend. Close your eyes. Feel the soles of your feet connecting with the earth.

Now bring your awareness to your breath. Inhale slowly through your nose... and exhale gently through your mouth. With each exhale, let your shoulders drop a little more. Let your jaw soften. Let your belly be soft.

You are arriving in this moment. There is nowhere else to be. Feel the weight of your body sinking down. Feel the sky above you. You are held between heaven and earth.

Stay here for a few breaths. When you feel centered, gently open your eyes.

------------------------------------------------
EXERCISE 2: LIFTING THE SKY
Duration: 5 minutes
------------------------------------------------

Begin with arms relaxed at your sides.

As you inhale slowly, begin to raise both arms in front of you, palms facing the ground, fingers relaxed and slightly apart. Let the movement be so slow — as if you are moving through water. Arms rising... rising...

When your hands reach shoulder height, continue lifting. Now turn your palms to face the sky. Gently press your palms upward toward the ceiling. Feel the stretch along your sides, through your ribs, into your armpits. Hold this stretch for one breath.

Now as you exhale, slowly lower your arms back down in front of you, palms still facing up. Let them float down like feathers. Return to your starting position.

Let's do this again. Inhale, arms rising slowly... palms turning up at the top... gentle stretch... and exhale, arms floating down.

One more time. Inhale... lifting the sky... feeling your whole body lengthen... and exhale... returning home.

Lower your arms completely. Feel the energy in your palms.

------------------------------------------------
EXERCISE 3: PARTING THE WILD HORSE'S MANE
Duration: 5 minutes
------------------------------------------------

Step your right foot forward into a gentle bow stance — front knee soft, back leg straight but not locked.

Now extend your right arm forward, palm facing out, as if you are gently pushing something away. At the same time, your left hand pulls back beside your hip, palm facing down. Look forward past your right hand. Feel the gentle stretch across your chest.

Now slowly shift your weight to your back leg. As you do, your hands switch — left hand comes forward, right hand pulls back. Let the movement be continuous, like a slow wave flowing through your body.

Shift forward again... and back... forward... and back. Your eyes follow the leading hand. Feel the gentle rotation in your waist and spine.

This is a meditation in motion. There is no rush. Each shift is an opportunity to feel your body more deeply.

Now step your feet back together. Pause. Feel the warmth in your palms.

Now step the left foot forward and repeat on the other side. Left hand forward, right hand back... shifting weight slowly... feeling the wave move through you.

------------------------------------------------
EXERCISE 4: WAVE HANDS LIKE CLOUDS
Duration: 6 minutes
------------------------------------------------

Feet shoulder-width apart, knees soft. Begin with both hands at your sides.

Now slowly shift your weight to your left foot. As you do, your right hand rises in front of you, palm facing your chest, at about heart height. Your left hand rests near your left hip, palm facing down.

Now begin to shift your weight to the right. As you do, your hands begin to trace a gentle arc — the right hand descends while the left hand rises. Your hands pass each other at about navel height, palms facing each other but not touching.

Continue the arc — left hand rising to heart height on the right side, right hand descending to the hip. Now shift left again, and the hands reverse. Right hand rises... left hand descends... passing each other... continuing the wave.

Your whole body moves together — weight shifting, waist turning gently, hands tracing clouds. Let your eyes follow the upper hand. Breathe naturally. Inhale as the hand rises... exhale as it descends.

This movement should feel like a gentle, continuous wave. There are no sharp edges. Everything flows.

Keep going... side to side... like clouds drifting... like water flowing... your body is the sky and your hands are the clouds.

After several repetitions, slowly bring your hands to rest at your sides. Stand quietly and feel the energy moving through you.

------------------------------------------------
EXERCISE 5: HOLDING THE BALLOON
Duration: 5 minutes
------------------------------------------------

Begin standing with feet shoulder-width apart. Now bring your hands together in front of your chest, palms facing each other, about shoulder-width apart. Imagine you are holding a large, warm balloon between your palms. Feel the gentle energy between your hands.

Now slowly shift the balloon to the right — your whole upper body turns gently to the right, left heel lifting slightly. Hold here for a breath. Feel the stretch along your left side.

Now slowly shift the balloon to the left. Right heel lifts. Hold. Breathe.

Now bring the balloon back to center. This time, slowly raise it overhead — palms still facing each other, arms extending upward. Feel the stretch through your entire body, from your feet to your fingertips. Hold for two breaths.

Now slowly lower the balloon back to chest height. Turn it to the right... to the left... and now gently set it down, hands lowering to your sides.

Close your eyes for a moment. Feel the warmth and tingling in your palms. This is your Qi — your life force energy.

------------------------------------------------
EXERCISE 6: CRANE SPREADS ITS WINGS
Duration: 5 minutes
------------------------------------------------

Begin by shifting your weight onto your left foot. Find your balance. Let your right foot lift slightly off the ground, knee bent.

Now slowly extend both arms out to the sides at shoulder height, palms facing down, fingers relaxed. You are a crane standing in still water.

Now gently lean your torso forward as your right leg extends behind you. Keep your back straight — imagine a line from your head to your back foot. Your arms spread wide like wings. Hold this position for two slow breaths. Feel the balance, the openness in your chest, the strength in your standing leg.

Now slowly return to upright, bringing your right knee back up and arms back to your sides. Pause. Feel your heartbeat.

Now repeat on the other side. Weight shifts to the right foot. Left knee lifts. Arms extend... torso leans forward... left leg extends behind... crane spreading its wings. Hold... breathe... and return.

Step your feet together. Stand quietly. Feel the balance you have cultivated.

------------------------------------------------
EXERCISE 7: CLOSING MEDITATION — GATHERING QI
Duration: 4 minutes
------------------------------------------------

Stand with feet together or shoulder-width apart. Close your eyes. Bring your hands to rest in front of your lower abdomen — about two inches below your navel. This is your dantian, the center of your life force energy. Palms facing your body, fingers pointing down, hands about six inches apart.

Breathe deeply into this area. With each inhale, imagine golden light gathering in your dantian. With each exhale, feel this light warming and filling your lower belly.

Now slowly raise your hands in front of you as you inhale — palms facing up — as if you are lifting a ball of light from your dantian up through your center line. When your hands reach chest height, turn your palms down and slowly press them back down as you exhale — pushing the energy back into your dantian.

Inhale, lifting the light... exhale, pressing it down.

One more time. Inhale... gathering all the energy from today's practice... exhale... sealing it deep within you.

Now place both palms flat against your lower abdomen. Hold here. Breathe naturally. Feel the warmth. Feel the stillness.

You have awakened your body. You have cultivated your Qi. Carry this energy with you into your day.

When you are ready, gently open your eyes. Bring your palms together in front of your heart. Bow your head slightly. Thank yourself for showing up today. Namaste.

================================================

TIPS FOR RECORDING:
- Use a warm, calm voice. Think meditation guide, not fitness instructor.
- Pause at every "..." — these are breath marks.
- Speak slightly slower than normal conversation (0.85x speed setting).
- Keep volume consistent — no sudden loud phrases.
- Record in a quiet room with minimal reverb.
- MP3 at 128kbps or WAV for best quality.

TTS TOOLS (ranked by quality):
1. ElevenLabs (elevenlabs.io) — best quality, free tier ~10k chars/month
2. PlayHT (playht.com) — very good, free trial available
3. Azure Neural TTS (speech.microsoft.com) — good, free demo available
4. OpenAI TTS (tts-1-hd) — very good if you have API key
5. Edge TTS (built into Hermes) — decent, free, unlimited

For this script (~3500 chars), any of these will work within free tiers.
