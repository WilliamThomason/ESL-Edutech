📖 Past Simple — Quick Reminder

We use the Past Simple for actions that started and finished in the past.

✅ Regular verbs: add -ed (played, walked, cooked)
✅ Irregular verbs: special forms (went, ate, saw, did)
✅ Negative: didn't + base verb (didn't go, didn't eat)
