How does the free trial work?▼
Book a free 30-minute session with one of our teachers. We'll assess your level, discuss your goals, and show you how the platform works. No payment required.
What exams do you prepare for?▼
We cover IELTS, TOEFL, OET, CAE, CFEFS, CFE, IGCSE, Cambridge exams, PTE, Duolingo English Test, and CELPIP. If it's a major English exam, we have materials and strategies for it.
How does AI translation work during lessons?▼
When a concept can't be explained in English alone, your teacher can instantly translate key terms into your home language using integrated AI tools. This saves you from having to look up words after class and keeps the lesson flowing.
What is the Beta Pioneer offer?▼
The first 200 students to sign up get the Professional plan ($279/mo value) for just $99/mo, locked for 12 months. You also get early access to new features and direct input on platform development.
Do you offer corporate training?▼
Yes. We offer custom corporate contracts starting at $2,500/month with dedicated account managers, bulk pricing, custom content, and team progress dashboards. Request a proposal above.
How does the referral program work?▼
Share your unique link. When a friend books their free trial, you both get $50 credit. Refer 3 friends and earn 1 free month. Refer 5 and earn 3 free months.
What does FL0-FL13 mean?▼
Our proprietary Fluency Level system ranges from FL0 (absolute beginner) to FL13 (near-native). Every level has clear milestones, assessments, and learning outcomes so you always know exactly where you stand.
