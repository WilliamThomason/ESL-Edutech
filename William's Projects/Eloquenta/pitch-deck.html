The Problem

English education is broken for non-native speakers

😰 Students Are Lost

Most ESL platforms teach English in English. Students who don't understand feel embarrassed, fall behind, and quit. In Japan, Korea, Germany, Spain, Brazil — millions of potential learners are excluded.

📉 High Dropout Rates

Online ESL platforms see 60-80% dropout within 3 months. Why? Impersonal content, no emotional connection, and lessons that don't adapt to the student's actual goals.

🌍 One Size Fits None

A Japanese student preparing for TOEFL has completely different needs than a German professional working in tech. Current platforms offer the same content to everyone.

💸 Premium = Exclusive

Quality 1-on-1 tutoring costs $40-80/hour. Group classes are affordable but impersonal. There's no middle ground — quality education for the masses.

The Solution

Eloquenta — Premium ESL that speaks your language

🎯 Goal-Focused Learning

Every lesson is built around the student's specific goal — TOEFL, IELTS, OET, IGCSE, Business English, accent reduction, or interview prep. No generic content.

🗣️ Teach in Their Language

Our teachers speak the student's native language. German, Spanish, Japanese — explanations happen in the language they understand best. This is our unfair advantage.

🤖 AI + Human Hybrid

Interactive AI lesson slides for self-study + live 1-on-1 sessions with professional tutors. The AI handles practice and repetition. The human handles nuance and motivation.

📱 Free Live Sessions

Weekly free live sessions on popular topics (exam tips, pronunciation, grammar) let students experience our teaching style before committing. This is our growth engine.

Growth Strategy

Free education as our acquisition engine

📺 Free Live Sessions

Weekly free live sessions on YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok. Topics like "5 TOEFL Mistakes Japanese Students Make" or "Business English for German Engineers." Students experience our teaching style → convert to paid.

📱 Social Media Campaigns

Students get discounts for social media engagement: share a post → 10% off, refer a friend → 20% off, create a TikTok review → 1 month free. Viral growth loop built into the product.

🤝 Ambassador Program

Top students become Eloquenta ambassadors. They get free access + commission for every referral. We target university students in Japan, Germany, Spain, and Australia.

Partner with language schools, universities, and corporations. They get our AI platform + teacher network. We get their students + credibility. Revenue share model.
