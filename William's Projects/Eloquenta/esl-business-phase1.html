1.2 Market Research 5 days

▼

EU market analysis Research

Research demand in Germany, France, Spain, Italy. Key search terms: "English tutor online", "English lessons [city]". Note average prices. Identify gaps (translation support, business English).

Asia market analysis Research

Research Japan, South Korea, China. Focus on TOEFL/IELTS demand. Note: Japanese market values structure and grammar. Korean market is exam-driven. Chinese market is huge but competitive.

South America market analysis Research

Research Brazil, Colombia, Argentina. Business English demand is high. Price sensitivity is higher. WhatsApp is the primary communication channel.

Create market research doc Document

Compile findings into a single document: market size, average prices, key competitors, gaps/opportunities, recommended pricing per region.

0/4 done

1.4 Translation Workflow Design 3 days

▼

Define translation approach Decision

How will live translations work during lessons? Options: (a) Teacher speaks target language directly, (b) Teacher provides written translations in chat, (c) Hybrid. Document the workflow.

Create glossary template Template

Design the translation glossary structure: English term → German → Spanish → French → Italian → Portuguese → Japanese → Korean → Chinese. Start with FL0-FL1 common terms.

Test DeepL for initial translations Research

Sign up for DeepL API (free tier). Test translation quality for common ESL terms in all 8 languages. Document quality issues and manual review needs.

Write translation QA process Document

Document the workflow: AI translates → You review DE/ES → Hire native reviewers for FR/IT/PT/JA/KO/ZH → Final glossary. Estimate time per language per level.
