THE TAX RESIDENCE QUESTION (Most Important)

If you're a South African tax resident, SA taxes your worldwide income regardless of where your company is registered. Registering in UAE or Estonia doesn't automatically reduce your SA tax bill.

To truly benefit from a low-tax jurisdiction, you'd need to either:

  • Become non-resident for SA tax purposes (emigrate formally — "financial emigration")
  • Or use the foreign employment income exemption (if you're physically outside SA for 183+ days/year)

→ Talk to a SA cross-border tax accountant. This single decision affects everything.
