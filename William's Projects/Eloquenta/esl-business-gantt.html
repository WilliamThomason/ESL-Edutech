Student Booking Flow

1. Student visits your site → clicks "Book a Lesson"
2. Cal.com widget shows your availability (synced from Google Calendar)
3. Student picks slot + lesson type + enters name/email/language
4. On confirmation: Google Calendar event + Meet link + Talky.io room auto-generated
5. Student dashboard shows "Join Lesson" button 15 min before
6. On click: Meet loads first → JS health check → Talky.io auto-highlighted if fail
