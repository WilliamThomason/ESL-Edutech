Our Curriculum

14 levels. CEFR-aligned. From zero to proficient.

FL0-FL2

Beginner — Pre-A1 to A1+

Objects, nationality, daily routine, present simple, past simple basics

  • To be, have/has
  • Present & past simple
  • Numbers, dates, time
FL3

Pre-Intermediate — A2

Obligation, future plans, comparisons, phrasal verbs, shopping, weather

  • Must/should
  • Going to / will
  • Comparative & superlative
FL4-FL5

Intermediate — B1

Present perfect, conditionals, passive voice, phone calls, tech

  • Present perfect & continuous
  • Passive voice
  • Relative clauses, modals
FL6-FL7

Upper Intermediate — B2

All past tenses, 3rd conditional, reported speech, job interviews

  • Past perfect, 3rd conditional
  • Reported speech
  • Work phrasal verbs
FL8-FL10

Advanced — C1

Mixed conditionals, passive + modals, connectors, presentations

  • Mixed & advanced conditionals
  • Advanced connectors
  • Business English & presentations
FL11-FL13

Proficient — C2 / C2+

Debates, data, societal issues, custom topics

  • Economics & politics
  • Media & marketing
  • Fully customized content

Your free trial isn't just a taste — it's the foundation of your entire learning path. Here's how we use it to build a course that's uniquely yours.

📋 What happens in your 20-minute speaking assessment:
🎯 Fluency & Accuracy We measure your speaking speed, hesitation patterns, and error frequency to pinpoint exactly where you struggle.
🗣️ Pronunciation We identify specific sounds, stress patterns, and intonation issues that are unique to your first language.
📚 Vocabulary Range We assess your active vs. passive vocabulary, and identify the gaps between what you understand and what you can produce.
🎪 Goals & Context We learn your real-world needs — work presentations, daily conversation, exams, travel — so every lesson has purpose.
🔬 How we build your custom course:
1.Your assessment data generates a personal fluency profile — a detailed map of your strengths and weaknesses across 5 dimensions.
2.We match you with a teacher who specializes in your specific needs — whether that's business English, exam prep, or everyday fluency.
3.Your curriculum is built around your goals, not a generic textbook. Every exercise, every conversation topic, every piece of homework is chosen for you.
4.We re-assess every 4 weeks and adjust your plan. As you improve, the course evolves with you.
