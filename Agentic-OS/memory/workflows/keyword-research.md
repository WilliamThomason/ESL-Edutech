# Workflow: Keyword Research → Content → Publish

## Trigger
New content request or monthly content calendar

## Steps
1. **Researcher** — Generate keyword list (20-50 keywords)
2. **Researcher** — Analyze competitor content for each keyword
3. **Writer** — Draft article for highest-priority keyword
4. **Reviewer** — Quality check, brand voice, SEO optimization
5. **Analyst** — Verify keyword targeting, meta tags, internal links
6. **Publish** — Deploy to CMS
7. **Analyst** — Track rankings after 7, 14, 30 days

## Token Budget
- Researcher: 50K
- Writer: 30K
- Reviewer: 10K
- Analyst: 10K
- **Total: ~100K per article**

## Output
- Published article URL
- Keyword ranking tracker entry
- Content calendar update
