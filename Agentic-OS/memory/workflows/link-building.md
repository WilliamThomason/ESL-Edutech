# Workflow: Link Building Outreach

## Trigger
Monthly link building campaign or new client onboarding

## Steps
1. **Researcher** — Find 50 prospect websites (DA 20+, relevant niche)
2. **Outreach** — Personalize email templates for each prospect
3. **Outreach** — Send initial emails (batch of 10/day)
4. **Outreach** — Follow-up after 5 days (non-responders)
5. **Outreach** — Follow-up after 10 days (second attempt)
6. **Analyst** — Verify placed links, update tracker
7. **Analyst** — Measure traffic impact after 30 days

## Token Budget
- Researcher: 40K
- Outreach: 30K
- Analyst: 10K
- **Total: ~80K per campaign**

## Output
- Outreach spreadsheet updated
- Link tracker updated
- Monthly backlink report
