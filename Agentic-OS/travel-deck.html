Travel Smart

01

Book accommodation early

Prices rise 15-30% within 2 weeks of peak dates. Book 4-6 weeks ahead for best rates.

02

Read recent reviews

Photos can be outdated. Reviews from the last 3 months tell the real story of a place.

03

Consider location over luxury

A 3-star hotel in the city center beats a 5-star in the suburbs. You're there to explore, not sit in a lobby.

04

Travel in shoulder season

May-June and September-October offer the best balance of good weather, fewer crowds, and lower prices.
